## Why CommonSquare

**Section label:** Why CommonSquare

**Heading:** Ideas deserve a fair fight

**Subheading:** Right now, ideas are judged by who says them — not whether they're good. We think that should change.

### Card 1 — Ideas Over Identity
No party badges. No team jerseys. Just people making their best case on the issues that matter — and letting the audience decide.

### Card 2 — Watch, Vote, or Debate
You don't have to debate to be part of it. Watch real people go head-to-head on video, vote on who made the stronger case, or step in yourself when you're ready.

### Card 3 — The Best Ideas Rise
Elo rankings, audience voting, and structured rounds mean the strongest arguments win — regardless of who's making them.

## How It Works

**Section label:** How It Works

**Heading:** Three steps to claim your spot

**Subheading:** Create your profile, discover where you stand, and be first in line when the arena opens.

### Step 1 — Create Your Profile (30 seconds)
Choose your username and claim your spot on CommonSquare. Your profile is your identity in the arena — lock it in before someone else does.

### Step 2 — Discover Your Compass (2 minutes)
Answer 30 questions to see where you land on the political spectrum. You'll get your personal compass and debate archetype — from Modern Moderate to Constitutional Conservative.

### Step 3 — Enter the Arena (Coming soon)
When the platform launches, you're first in line. Watch debates, vote on the best arguments, or jump in and compete yourself.
